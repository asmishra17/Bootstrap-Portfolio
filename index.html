    About Me

    Hello! Welcome to my page. My name is Aditi Mishra, and I was born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area. I graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a degree in Economics and Applied Math before going on to complete the Leadership Development Training Program at MB Financial Bank in Chicago, where I currently work.

    Now, I am in MB's Business Intelligence group while working toward a certificate in Web Development from Northwestern University. Outside of work and school, I serve on the "Chicago Alliance Against Sexual Exploitation's" board, and I enjoy exploring new restaurants, traveling, cooking, and listening to Podcasts.
